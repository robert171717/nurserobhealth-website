Every thread follows the same arc:
- Tweet 1: Shocking hook ("BPC-157 healed a rat's eye after CUT through cornea with surgical knife")

- Tweet 3: "What even is [X]?" origin/backstory

- Tweet 6-8: Specific use cases (LASIK dry eye, etc.)

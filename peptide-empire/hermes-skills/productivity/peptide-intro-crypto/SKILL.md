# Peptide Intro for Crypto Folks

## Core Analogy
Peptides = short amino acid chains that act as signaling molecules. DNA is the blueprint, proteins are the building, **peptides are the foreman** — they tell cells what to do.

## The Crypto ↔ Peptide Bridge

| Crypto Mindset | Peptide Parallel |
|---|---|
| Self-custody | Self-sovereign health. You research, source, and manage your own protocol. |
| Early alpha | Peptide space is pre-mainstream. GLP-1s are just wave one. |
| Asymmetric upside | $50 vial of BPC-157 can fix what years of doctors couldn't. |
| DYOR culture | PubChem, PubMed, community anecdotes, self-experimentation. |

## Major Categories
- **Healing/Recovery** — BPC-157, TB-500 (gut, tendon, injury repair)
- **Longevity/Skin** — GHK-Cu (skin, hair, anti-aging)
- **Metabolic/Weight** — GLP-1s: semaglutide, tirzepatide (Ozempic family)
- **Cognition** — Semax, Selank, Cerebrolysin (nootropics)
- **Growth/Repair** — CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin (GH secretagogues)

## Key Reality Check
Most peptides are NOT FDA-approved for the uses people take them for. They're research chemicals in a gray zone. That's the risk — and the edge.

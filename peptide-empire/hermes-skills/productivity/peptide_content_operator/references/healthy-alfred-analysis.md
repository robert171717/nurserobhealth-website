## Thread Structure (Reverse-Engineered)

1. **Dramatic Hook** — shocking claim, no image
2. **Education** — "what even is X?" plain English
3. **Evidence Bomb** — research findings + **4 PubMed screenshots** (most bookmarked tweet)
4. **Specific Example** — one clinical scenario
5. **Another Example** — different angle
6. **Citation Dump** — PMIDs, no images

## What NOT to Copy

- **Affiliate codes:** Alfred uses "Code ALFRED" for Barrier Health and Greco Gum. Nurse Rob has no equivalent. Must be stripped from any modeled content.
- **"Your Doctor Is Wrong" framing:** Works for a non-credentialed account. For a licensed RN, this undermines clinical credibility. Replace with "Here's what the research actually shows."
- **Shipping/selling language:** Alfred positions himself as a product recommender. Nurse Rob positions himself as an educator. Crossing into sales language without infrastructure is a liability.

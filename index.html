What is the Wolverine Stack?

428|

The Wolverine Stack combines BPC-157 and TB-500 — two research-backed peptides studied for tissue repair and recovery. BPC-157 targets localized healing in the GI tract and soft tissue, while TB-500 works systemically to promote cell migration and angiogenesis. There is zero human safety data on stacking them together — this is theoretical synergy from animal models. Always consult your physician before considering any peptide protocol.

What's the difference between peptide education and medical advice?

432|

As a licensed RN, I provide educational consulting — I explain what the research shows, walk through dosing protocols from published studies, and help you understand potential risks. I do not prescribe, diagnose, or treat. No provider-patient relationship is formed. Think of it as having a clinically-trained guide who reads the studies so you don't have to.

Which peptides are best for anti-aging?

436|

GHK-Cu has the strongest evidence for skin regeneration and collagen synthesis. Ipamorelin and CJC-1295 are growth hormone secretagogues studied for body composition. Semaglutide and Tirzepatide are GLP-1 agonists with significant metabolic and longevity data. Each has distinct mechanisms, risks, and evidence quality. This is exactly what I cover in a 1-on-1 protocol review — matching compounds to your specific goals and health profile.

How do I know my peptide source is legitimate?

440|

This is the most important question in the peptide space. Look for third-party COAs (Certificates of Analysis) from labs like Janoshik or MZ Biolabs. Verify the batch number matches. Check for endotoxin testing, not just purity. Avoid any source that won't share test results. In a consultation, I can help you evaluate what you're considering — but I do not endorse or recommend specific vendors.

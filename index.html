ABOUT

Why an RN is different from a "biohacking guru"

I'm Nurse Rob — a licensed registered nurse who got tired of watching people inject substances they didn't fully understand. The peptide space is full of influencers who've never set foot in a hospital, never read a clinical trial cover-to-cover, and never had a patient's safety on their conscience.

My training includes pharmacology, pathophysiology, and years of direct patient care. When I evaluate a peptide protocol, I'm looking at it through the same clinical lens I'd use for any treatment plan: What does the evidence actually show? What are the real risks? What are we still unsure about?

I'm not here to sell you peptides. I'm here to help you understand them — safely, honestly, and based on real science.
